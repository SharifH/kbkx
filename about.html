About KabuK Exchange

Travel distribution infrastructure by KabuK Style Inc.

KabuK Exchange is a travel distribution infrastructure product by KabuK Style Inc. It is designed to connect hotels, distributors, agencies, and technology partners for scalable B2B2B travel commerce.

Our mission

Our mission

Our mission is to connect Japanese hospitality supply with global demand. We want hotels and hotel groups — from independent ryokan to urban brands — to reach travelers worldwide, while giving distribution and technology partners a modern, reliable way to access that supply.

KabuK Exchange is designed as shared infrastructure: a common platform where supply and demand meet through APIs, with the operational tooling needed to make multi-party travel commerce work at scale.

A note on capabilities

What is live vs. planned

KabuK Exchange is evolving. Some capabilities described across this site are live today, while others — including PMS/channel manager connectivity and AI-assisted onboarding, support, and pricing — are planned or on our roadmap. We aim to be clear about the difference and will not overstate current live capabilities.
